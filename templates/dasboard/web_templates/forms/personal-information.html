NEXT OF KIN INFORMATION

The Red Army Foundation asks that you provide a Next of Kin as part of your Membership Application Process. The “Next of Kin” refers to a person's closest living relative, including those with blood relationships and legally adopted relatives, such as a spouse, parents, siblings, and children.

The membership database the Red Army Foundation is developing is a historical factual document and can be used to obtain benefits and privileges from the Red Army Foundation, its institutions, partners, and governments.

Therefore, the foundation would like to ensure that every member registered can be traced when their contacts are no longer in service. Hence, the foundation asks that you accurately designate one person among your relatives as the next of kin and answer the required questions.

Any membership or privileges awarded based on false information you provided will be canceled. The Membership Disciplinary Committee shall impose disciplinary actions on those who knowingly provided incorrect information.
