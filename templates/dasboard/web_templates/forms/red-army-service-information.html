RED ARMY SERVICE INFORMATION

This part of the Membership Application form is limited to “ORGANIZED RED ARMY” as dictated by the Constitution of the Red Army Foundation. Organized Red Army, according to the constitution and membership classification, refers to those Red Army who were formally organized under Military (e.g., Bilpam, Bonga, Lotuke, Moli, Panda Oyee, Achodok, Moroto), Refugee (e.g., Pinyudu, Dimma, Tharpam, Kakuma, Adjumani), IDP (e.g., Polotaka, Narus, Natinga, Moli, Lobone, Aswa), and Diaspora (e.g., Cuba) camps during the SPLM/A Liberation Struggle for freedom.
